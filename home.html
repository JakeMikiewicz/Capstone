Welcome to the Fairmont Community Partnership Group!

The Fairmont Community Partnership is an organized incorporation primarily focused on enhancing the lives of the residents of Fairmont by being a one stop shop for a variety of services, employment opportunities, and community events. The inspiration for the founding of the partnership group was championed largely by Delinda Herod, a resident of the community herself.
